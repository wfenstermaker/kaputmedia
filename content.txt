Kaput is an arts and media consultancy in Los Angeles, offering editorial consulting and content services to artists, galleries, museums, cultural organizations and luxury brands. Services include artist statements, press materials, institutional copy and editorial strategy. 

Kaput is founded and run by Will Fenstermaker, a writer and cultural critic whose essays on contemporary art, visual culture and media theory are published by _Artforum_, _BOMB_, _Dissent_, _Family Style_, _The Los Angeles Review of Books_, _The Los Angeles Times_, _The Nation_, _The Paris Review Daily_ and _T Magazine_, among other publications. Fenstermaker’s editorial experience includes positions at Sotheby’s, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, _Frieze_ and _The Brooklyn Rail_, and he is a recipient of The Andy Warhol Foundation Arts Writers Grant.
