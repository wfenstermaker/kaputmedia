Kaput

Criticism for culture coming apart

Founded by Will Fenstermaker

Kaput is an arts and media consultancy in Los Angeles, offering publishing and editorial services to artists, galleries, museums, cultural organizations and luxury brands. Services include artist statements, press materials, institutional copy and editorial strategy.

Kaput is founded and run by Will Fenstermaker, a writer and cultural critic whose essays on contemporary art, visual culture and media theory are published by Artforum, BOMB, Dissent, Family Style, The Los Angeles Review of Books, The Los Angeles Times, The Nation, The Paris Review Daily and T Magazine, among other publications. Fenstermaker's editorial experience includes positions at Sotheby's, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, Frieze and The Brooklyn Rail, and he is a recipient of The Andy Warhol Foundation Arts Writers Grant.
